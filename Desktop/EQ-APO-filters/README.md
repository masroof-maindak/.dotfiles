# EQ APO Filters

## Tangzu Wan'er

- Honestly, I think these might just be more fun w/o any EQ, and just reducing
  the bass ever so slightly would be more fruitful
- After a couple of days w/ them, I like stock more than even Serenade or Harman
  IE 2019
  - What I need to achieve is the 'average' of Harman IE 2019 (or Serenade) and
    stock

### Super22

- Didn't like it at all

### Harman IE 2019

- Settled on Harman IE 2019, obtained from squig.link
- Better spoken word clarity
- Treble/air is a tad bit too high
  - Still better on average
  - Figure out what band produces the 'hiss'/sharp sounds, and reduce it
    ever-so-slightly
  - I _believe_ it should be around the 10k range, i.e between mid treble &
    air/upper treble frequencies; since that's where I see the largest
    disconnect b/w Harman's curve and that of the stock Tangzu tuning

### Seranade

> It's no secret that I'm in love with female vocals, especially ones that leans
> on sounding delicate/ angelic. Seranade is the cleanest possible EQ I can
> think of to highlight that vocal quality while keeping as much of the original
> Wan'er gain profile as possible. Recommended Genre: R&B, Anime OST, K-Pop,
> J-Pop
